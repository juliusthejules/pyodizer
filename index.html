Pyodizer™

A Python-based privacy and security Progressive Web App (PWA)

What is Pyodizer™?

Pyodizer™ is a Python-based PWA designed to enhance privacy and security by modifying key configurations on your device. It anonymizes device information, blocks both internal & external access, and enhances encryption, making your setup more secure.

Key Features

  • Device Anonymization: Disables the MAC address to prevent device tracking.
  • IP Address Management: Blocks both internal & external IP access by setting IPv4 and IPv6 addresses to localhost.
  • Firewall Rules: Configures firewall rules to block all ports and closes pings for preventing unwanted traffic. Blocks tracerouting to stop all tracking.
  • DNS & Hostname Configuration: Ensures DNS and hostname are set to localhost for enhanced privacy.
  • Cookies: Clears existing cookies and blocks new cookies to prevent even more forms of tracking.
  • Cache: All cache is temporary and loads only the most important resources for the Pyodizer™ PWA to function.
  • Hyperlinks: All hyperlink rel's are set to 'noopener noreferrer' for maximum security.
  • Telemetry Disabled: Disables telemetry to prevent data collection.
  • Encryption: Enables AES-256 encryption to secure communications and data.
  • Fingerprinting: Obsfucates fingerprints to hide your browser details.
  • Automated Bot: Comes with a bot that automates the tasks of Pyodizer's anti-tracking features.

How to Install the Pyodizer™ PWA

  1. Open this page in a compatible browser on your device.
  2. Tap the menu icon in your browser (typically three dots or lines in the top right corner).
  3. Select “Add to Home Screen.”
  4. Tap Install. This will install Pyodizer™ as a PWA on your device.

Setting Up Permissions for Background Functionality

  1. After installation, open the Pyodizer™ app from your home screen.
  2. Enable permissions for notifications and background activity in your device's "App" settings.
  3. If additional permissions are required, navigate to your device’s settings, locate Pyodizer™, and turn on (enable):
    • Notifications (also enable the notification dot)
    • Unlimited mobile background data usage (mobile devices)
    • Unrestricted battey usage (mobile devices)
  4. Close the app and reopen it to apply the permissions settings.
